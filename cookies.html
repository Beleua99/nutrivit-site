Cookie Policy

Last updated May 12, 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners.

This Cookie Policy explains how Nutrivit (operated by The Creative Studio SRL) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on www.nutrivit.app.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly
  • Understand how visitors interact with our website (analytics)
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Remember your cookie preferences

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Provider Type Purpose Duration
_fbp Meta (Facebook) Marketing Used by Meta to deliver advertisements and track conversions from Facebook ads 90 days
_fbc Meta (Facebook) Marketing Stores the last ad click attribution for Meta ad campaigns 90 days
_ga Google Analytics Analytics Used to distinguish users and track sessions on Google Analytics 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics Analytics Used to persist session state across page requests 2 years
nv_cookie_consent Nutrivit Essential Stores your cookie consent preference so we don't show the banner on every visit 1 year

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We use the following third-party services:

  • Meta Pixel — used to measure the effectiveness of our advertising and to build audiences for our ads on Facebook and Instagram. You can opt out of Meta's targeted advertising at facebook.com/settings.
  • Google Analytics — used to understand how visitors interact with our website. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner where you can accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can also manage or withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. To find out more about cookies and how to manage them, visit aboutcookies.org.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Your Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, including data collected via cookies. For more information, please see our Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our data practices. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
